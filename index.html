Biography

I obtained my Ph.D. in June, 2024 at Division of Robotics, Perception, and Learning, EECS School, KTH, under the supervision of Prof. John Folkesson and Dr. Nils Bore, employed by Wallenberg AI, Autonomous Systems and Software Program (WASP).

My research interests are Robotics Perception, 3D Computer Vision and Deep Learning. My Ph.D. research focused on 3D reconstruction from imaging sonars and underwater Simultaneous Localization and Mapping (SLAM).
